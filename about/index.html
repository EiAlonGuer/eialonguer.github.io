About this portfolio

This page includes some projects I wanted to do to keep my technical skills on point while getting familiar with new tools and topics.

The work emphasis is on the numbers behind each project, trying to make the most out of my pre-existing skillset while also exploring environments, methods and data sources that might be outside the average academic project's scope.

I will try to keep a steady progress going on these projects as I get a deeper knowledge of the topics (and as I manage to find time), with the main goals being functionality and thematic exploration, over aesthetic perfection or mirroring work I do for other reasons.



As of now (9/3/26) there are three working projects, focusing on electric resource management, derivative pricing and market dynamics. These projects will likely be expanded in the future, while some objectives like reliable, high-quality data sourcing and processing are already fulfilled. Particular device compatibility problems affect UI sometimes, mostly only as small visual bugs, but that is for now a secondary concern.
